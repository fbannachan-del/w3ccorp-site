What we do

Payment technology, engineered as infrastructure.

The group designs, builds and operates the systems that let regulated businesses move money, from the rails underneath to the programs on top.

Payment Infrastructure

Core platforms and connectivity that underpin how money moves, designed for resilience, throughput and continuous availability.

Money Movement and Processing

Technology that authorizes, processes and settles transactions across accounts, cards and digital assets, with visibility end to end.

Program Enablement

The tooling and services partners use to launch and run payment programs, configurable, white-labeled and ready for their market.

Compliance and Oversight

Risk, monitoring and governance capabilities built into the platform, so programs meet the demands of the markets they operate in.

The Group

A group, built as infrastructure.

W3C Corp sits at the top of a group of specialised payment technology companies. Each operates in its own market and regulatory context. Together they share a common foundation of engineering, infrastructure and oversight.

  • One foundationShared infrastructure and standards across every operating company in the group.
  • Local by marketOperating companies positioned for the regulatory and commercial realities of their region.
  • Governed centrallyGroup-level oversight, risk and governance holding the whole structure to one standard.
